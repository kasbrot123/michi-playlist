About

This playlist of songs represents my full spectrum of genres I grew up with and continuing. I started as a teenager collecting songs, labelled them with tags like artist and album cover and put them on my apple ipod. Whenever I heard a song I liked, I added it to my playlist, there was no special band or full album. Just a pure selection of music connected by my taste. In this ancient time people cared more about the songs they listened to, not so in the present time, I think. I decided to put them all online, so I can hear them everywhere on every device. Therefore, it is easy to share with others, so happy you. Maybe you also find a hidden piece of nostalgia which puts you back to your early years.
